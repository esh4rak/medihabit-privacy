1. Introduction

Welcome to MediHabit. We are committed to protecting your personal health data. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data.

2. Information We Collect

MediHabit collects the following health data that you manually enter:

  • Blood pressure readings (systolic, diastolic, pulse)
  • Blood glucose readings (fasting, post-meal, random)
  • Body temperature readings
  • BMI data (height and weight)
  • Lipid profile readings (LDL, HDL, total cholesterol, triglycerides)
  • Medication information and reminder schedules

With your explicit permission, MediHabit also reads the following data from Health Connect

  • Step count
  • Distance walked
  • Calories burned

3. How We Use Your Information

All health data is used solely to:

  • Display your personal health history within the app
  • Show trends and charts of your health metrics
  • Send medication reminders you have configured
  • Calculate health indicators such as BMI and lipid ratios

4. Health Connect Data

MediHabit uses Android Health Connect to read your physical activity data. Specifically, we access step count, distance walked, and calories burned data. This data is displayed in the Pedometer feature of the app and is stored locally on your device only. It is never uploaded to any external server or shared with third parties.

Health Connect permissions are managed directly on your device. You can revoke access at any time by going to Health Connect â†’ App permissions â†’ MediHabit on your Android device.

Our use of Health Connect data complies with the Health Connect Permissions Policy and Android's data use requirements.

5. Data Sharing

We do NOT:

  • Share your data with any third parties
  • Use your data for advertising purposes
  • Sell your data to anyone
  • Upload your health data to any external server

6. Data Storage

All data entered in MediHabit is stored locally on your device using a secure local database. We do not operate any servers or cloud storage for your health data.

7. Data Deletion

You can delete all your data at any time by:

  • Deleting individual records within the app
  • Uninstalling the app, which removes all locally stored data

8. Children's Privacy

MediHabit is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.
