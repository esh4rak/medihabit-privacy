# Privacy Policy for MediHabit

Last updated: February 2026

## 1. Information We Collect

MediHabit collects the following health data that you manually enter:
- Blood pressure readings
- Blood glucose readings
- Body temperature readings
- BMI (height and weight)
- Lipid profile readings
- Medication information

With your permission, MediHabit also reads the following 
data from Google Fitness API:
- Step count
- Distance walked
- Calories burned

## 2. How We Use Your Information

All health data is used solely to:
- Display your personal health history within the app
- Show trends and charts of your health metrics
- Send medication reminders you have configured

We do NOT:
- Share your data with any third parties
- Use your data for advertising
- Sell your data to anyone
- Upload your data to any external server

## 3. Google Fitness API Data

Our use of Google Fitness API data is limited to displaying 
your step count, distance, and calorie information within 
the app. This data is stored locally on your device only.

Our use of information received from Google APIs adheres to 
the [Google API Services User Data Policy](https://developers.google.com/terms/api-services-user-data-policy), 
including the Limited Use requirements.

## 4. Data Storage

All data entered in MediHabit is stored locally on your 
device using an encrypted database. We do not operate 
any servers or cloud storage.

## 5. Data Deletion

You can delete all your data at any time by:
- Deleting individual records within the app
- Uninstalling the app, which removes all local data

## 6. Children's Privacy

MediHabit is not intended for children under 13 years of age.

## 7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. 
We will notify you of any changes by updating the 
"Last updated" date at the top of this page.
